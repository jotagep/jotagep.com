This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile applications ("Apps") published by **Jotagep** on the Google Play Store. It explains how we handle information in connection with our Apps.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to protecting your privacy. **We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data** or personally identifiable information from users of our Apps. We do not require registration or any user input to access the core features of the applications.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The Apps may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are the links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers that might be used by the Apps:

- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. Log Data and Cookies

Our Apps do not collect log data or cookies directly. However, the integrated third-party services (such as Google Play Services) may automatically collect standard device log information and usage statistics to analyze performance, troubleshoot errors, and improve application stability.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Since our Apps do not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you have reason to believe any information has been shared with us, please contact us immediately.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us via email at: [jotagepweb@gmail.com](mailto:jotagepweb@gmail.com).
